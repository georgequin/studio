# **App Name**: NHRC AI Watch

## Core Features:

- Image/PDF Upload & OCR: Allow staff to upload newspaper clippings (PDF or image) or capture images via mobile camera. extract text from images and PDFs.
- AI Text Extraction & Summarization: Use AI to extract and summarize the main content of each news story or article related to human rights violations. The LLM uses a tool to determine when the context of a clipping contains potential violations.
- Auto-Categorization: Automatically classify stories under predefined NHRC categories such as Communal Clashes, Terrorism, Women & Children, etc.
- Department Mapping: Automatically assign summaries to relevant NHRC departments based on the categorization (e.g., Women & Children department for stories about women and children).
- Dashboard View: Display a dashboard showing visual summaries of data: the number of stories per category, sources, and frequency.
- Report Export/Share: Enable the exporting of reports (PDF/CSV) categorized by issue and date. Also, allow sharing with UN organizations, NGOs, and internal teams.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Dark green to convey authority and trust.
- Background color: Light gray (#ECEFF1), a very low saturation version of the primary, to keep the focus on content.
- Accent color: Complementary orange (#FF9800) to highlight key actions and information, providing strong contrast against the blue.
- Body and headline font: 'Inter', a sans-serif font, for clear readability.
- Use simple, consistent icons from a library like Material Design Icons to represent categories and actions.
- Maintain a clean and structured layout with ample whitespace for improved readability and scannability.
- Incorporate subtle animations, like smooth transitions, to enhance user experience without being distracting.
